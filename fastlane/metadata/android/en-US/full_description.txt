Mako Scrubber removes hidden metadata — GPS location, camera make/model, timestamps, and software tags — from your photos before you share them.

Share a photo from any app to Mako Scrubber, and it hands you back a clean copy with that metadata stripped, ready to post or send. Batch-process multiple photos at once, and share or delete the cleaned copies right from the app. Cleaned photos are automatically removed after 30 days.

Mako Scrubber is local-only: it requests no INTERNET permission, so nothing ever leaves your device. No accounts, no cloud processing, no subscriptions.

Features:
* Strips GPS, camera make/model, timestamp, and software EXIF tags
* Works from any app's share sheet
* Batch scrub, share, and delete
* Zero INTERNET permission — verify it yourself in the manifest
* Free and open source (GPLv3)
